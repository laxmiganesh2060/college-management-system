About Us

üè´ Who We Are

Model Campus Damak (MCD), established in the year 2060 B.S., is the first private undergraduate college in Damak and Western Jhapa, to offer education in diverse disciplines like Management, Humanities, Education and Science. Since its establishment, MCD has been offering quality education to cater the needs and interest of Nepalese students, at the university level, allowing them to specialize in any of the subjects of their interest ‚Äì Accountancy, Finance, Marketing, Mathematics, English, Nepali, Rural Development, Sociology & Anthropology, Population Studies, Health & Physical Education, Literature, Journalism & Mass Communication, Poetry and so on. MCD has two sister concerns viz. Damak Model Higher Secondary School (DMHSS) and Motherland English Boarding School (MEBS).

üéØ Our Mission

Our Mission MCD DMHSS envisions to impart "Right Education" which is the blend of 3Cs ‚Äì Competency, Culture and Compassion, intended towards development of academic, civilized and responsible citizens who would contribute towards prosperous society and better world.

üî≠ Our Vision

Our Vision MCD & DMHSS strives to impart "Right Education" to suit the needs and interest of the students, in this digital age, at undergraduate and higher secondary level (10+2), by focusing in disciplines like Management, Science, Humanities and Education

Message from the Principal

Dear Students and Parents, I feel honored to have this opportunity to communicate my plan as the Principal of MCD & DMHSS. Having served the education sector for more than three decades, I have come to learn that holistic education is imperative...

Our education system should ensure physical, psychological and intellectual development of individuals to ensure desired quality in education. Quality in education can be achieved only through proper planning and implementation of appropriate education philosophy, guided by modern management practices.

Such practices help address the interests of all stakeholders ‚Äì students, parents, faculties, and society at large ‚Äì and ensure stronger teamwork. In this regard, MCD & DMHSS has introduced Counseling Services, Coordination Mechanism, Evaluation Mechanism, and Corporate Social Responsibility to support our academic mission.

Our Counseling Services help students overcome academic and personal problems and plan their future accordingly. The Coordination Mechanism minimizes centralized administrative complexities by decentralizing authority to program coordinators. The Evaluation Mechanism enhances student and faculty performance by tracking progress and suggesting necessary corrective actions. CSR aims to meet societal expectations by awarding scholarships based on merit and need.

I believe that the integration of our qualified and dedicated faculties, conducive environment, and modern management practices will yield the quality education you deserve. I invite you all to be part of our academic journey and take full advantage of the opportunities offered.

Best Regards,
